Golden Pothos

Description

Also known as Devil's Ivy, is a resilient and versatile houseplant with vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that often feature yellow or golden markings. . It's a low-maintenance plant, making it a favorite for both novice and experienced plant owners.

Golden Pothos

Care

It's often praised for its ability to thrive in low light and little maintenance, making it perfect for beginners or busy plant owners.

  • Light: Prefers bright, indirect light but can tolerate low light
  • Watering: Water when the top inch of soil is dry
  • Humidity: Prefers moderate to high humidity but adapts well to average indoor conditions.

Regular pruning helps to control the size and encourage bushier growth. Also, well-draining potting mix is ideal.

Fun Fact

The Golden Pothos loves to wrap around objects and climb whatever it can attach to.
