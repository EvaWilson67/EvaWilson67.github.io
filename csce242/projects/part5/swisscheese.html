Swiss Cheese Plant

Description

Often confused with its cousin, the larger Monstera deliciosa, but the Swiss Cheese Plant has smaller leaves and a more compact growth habit.

Swiss Cheese Plant

Care

The Swiss Cheese Plant is a great addition to any space, bringing a touch of the tropics into your home with its eye-catching leaves and easy care requirements.

  • Light: Prefers bright, indirect light but can tolerate lower light conditions
  • Watering: Water when the top inch of soil feels dry
  • Soil: Well-draining potting mix is ideal.

Feed with a balanced liquid fertilizer during the growing season (spring and summer) every 4-6 weeks. Also, prune back any leggy growth to maintain its shape and encourage fuller, bushier growth.
